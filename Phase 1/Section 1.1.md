**Assignment Title**: Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1

Create a sci-fi movie poster that takes place in a futuristic deep-sea research lab thousands of feet below the ocean. Scientists work inside the high-tech lab while looking through huge glass windows into the dark water, where a giant mysterious sea creature is slowly emerging from the depths. The poster uses sharp geometric lines from the lab's walls and equipment, along with beams of light cutting through the water to lead the viewer's eye toward the creature. Rectangular windows and circular control panels are the main shapes in the scene. The lab and scientists fill the foreground, while the vast dark ocean creates empty space that makes the setting feel lonely and isolated. Smooth metal walls, glass windows, and glowing screens contrast with the rough rocks and debris outside. The large research station and massive sea creature give the scene a strong sense of size and scale. A bright blue-white light shines on the creature against the dark ocean, creating a strong contrast that makes it the main focus. The overall mood is mysterious, tense, and exciting, capturing the fear and wonder of exploring the unknown.

**Vocabulary Breakdown**: Lines-Lines help guide your eyes through a picture and show where to look. Shape-Shapes are simple forms like circles, squares, and rectangles that make up an image. Texture-Texture is how something looks like it would feel, such as smooth, rough, or soft. Volume-Volume is how big or solid something looks, making it seem three-dimensional. Contrast-Contrast is the difference between things, like light and dark colors, that helps important parts stand out.
